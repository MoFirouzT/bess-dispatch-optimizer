## Objective

A **walk-forward backtest** over a BE/NL-shaped day-ahead price series that reports three revenue quantities ŌĆö a **greedy floor**, the **rolling deployable** value (per-day deterministic MILP), and the **perfect-foresight ceiling** (full-horizon MILP) ŌĆö plus the headline metric **% of perfect foresight captured**, solve-time stats, and constraint-satisfaction. Two correctness gates ride on it: a **leakage assertion** (no decision uses future prices) and a **sanity band** (annualized ceiling per MWh sits in a band derived from the series' own statistics).

The live ENTSO-E loader, multi-year pulls, and cross-year band re-validation are **R1.4b** ŌĆö out of scope here. R1.4a gates on a **synthetic** in-memory series so the suite runs **token-free and with no committed price data**.

### The greedy policy (precise, feasibility-preserving)

For each window's price block, with `p20`/`p80` the percentiles (pinned `method`):
1. **Charge** in period `t` iff `price[t] Ōēż p20` **and** there exists a later same-window period with `price Ōēź p80` still able to absorb the energy ŌĆö charge at the most the SoC headroom + power cap allow.
2. **Discharge** in period `t` iff `price[t] Ōēź p80` and SoC permits ŌĆö discharge at the most SoC + power cap allow.
3. **Otherwise idle.** A charge is additionally capped so the *trailing* periods can fully discharge it; any SoC left at window end is liquidated by force-discharging from the **final period backward** (keeping SoC Ōēź `e_min` throughout) so the window **ends empty** ŌĆö preserving the floor property `V_greedy Ōēż V_roll`.
Greedy respects all R1.1 physics (power caps, SoC bounds, efficiency in the SoC balance, mutual exclusion) by construction ŌĆö it is checked against the same property invariants, since it is **not** solver-produced.

## Golden oracles (exact expected values)

1 MWh / 1 MW asset, `dt=1`. Tolerance `1e-6`. Each "day" below is one window.

| # | inputs | greedy | rolling | ceiling | % captured | what it pins |
|---|--------|--------|---------|---------|-----------|--------------|
| 1 | 1 day `ŽĆ=[40,42]`, `╬Ę=0.95` | **ŌłÆ2.095** | **0.0** | **0.0** | n/a (`V*=0`) | greedy < rolling: greedy ignores the round-trip breakeven (5% spread < ~10.8%) and **trades at a loss**; MILP idles |
| 2 | 2 days `ŽĆ=[10,40 | 50,5]`, `╬Ę=1` | **30.0** | **30.0** | **40.0** | **75%** | rolling < ceiling: ceiling carries SoC **overnight** (buy@10 d1 ŌåÆ sell@50 d2, +40); rolling commits per-day (best is buy@10/sell@40 = 30, d2 no feasible trade) |
| 3 | 2 days flat `ŽĆ=[20,20 | 20,20]`, `╬Ę=1` | **0.0** | **0.0** | **0.0** | n/a | no spread ŌćÆ all three idle; degenerate `% captured` handled (`V*=0 ŌćÆ report 0`) |

**Oracle 1 (greedy loss).** `p20(method=linear)` of `[40,42] = 40.4`, `p80 = 41.6`. Greedy charges @40 (Ōēż40.4), discharges @42 (Ōēź41.6); grid-side it pays `40` to store `0.95`, sells `0.9025` @42 = `37.905`, net **ŌłÆ2.095**. The MILP sees `42/40ŌłÆ1 = 5% < 1/╬Ę^{rt}ŌłÆ1 Ōēł 10.8%` and idles ŌćÆ rolling = ceiling = **0**. Pins that optimization *avoids* losing trades a naive rule makes.

**Oracle 2 (overnight value).** Ceiling solves `[10,40,50,5]` full-horizon, `e0=e_end=0`, ╬Ę=1: buy@10, hold overnight, sell@50 ŌćÆ **40**. Rolling: day1 `[10,40]` ŌåÆ buy@10/sell@40 = **30**, end empty; day2 `[50,5]` ŌåÆ to start/end empty the only ordering is sell-then-buy (no energy to sell first) ŌćÆ **0**. So rolling = **30**, captured = 30/40 = **75%**, gap **10** = overnight arbitrage. Greedy day1 = buy@10/sell@40 = 30; day2 no later high after the low ŌćÆ idle ŌćÆ greedy = **30**.

## Property tests (invariants for any valid fixture)

- **Provable ordering:** `V_greedy Ōēż V_roll + ╬Ą` and `V_roll Ōēż V_ceiling + ╬Ą`, for random multi-day price series + valid specs (the core formulation gate of this phase).
- **Constraint satisfaction (all three baselines):** every concatenated schedule satisfies the R1.1 invariants ŌĆö power caps, mutual exclusion, SoC bounds, **exact** SoC continuity (efficiency grid-side), each window ends empty. Reuses the R1.1 property checks; especially guards **greedy** (not solver-produced).
- **Leakage (gate C):** perturbing prices strictly after window `i` leaves window `i`'s rolling schedule **unchanged** (decisions use only `╬Ā_d` + carried SoC). Structurally guaranteed by the windowing, asserted directly.
- **Objective consistency:** each baseline's reported `revenue_eur` equals the grid-side cash flow recomputed from its own schedule (`╬Ż ŽĆ┬Ędt┬Ę(p_dis ŌłÆ p_ch)`), no efficiency term.
- **Metric well-formed:** `pct_of_perfect_foresight Ōłł [0, 1+╬Ą]`; defined as `0` when `V* == 0`.
